Central Schemes: Policy & Impact

A professional index of key government policies, analyzed by objective and impact for quick reference and comparison. Each module provides essential data points for quick evaluation.

2015

Soil Health Card Scheme

Scientific Resource Management

Provides every farmer with a report detailing soil nutrient status across 12 parameters and precise fertilizer recommendations for responsible resource use.

Focus:

Productivity & Health

Analysis:

12 Soil Parameters

Outcome:

Reduced Chemical Use

Distribution:

Every 3 Years

2016

e-National Agriculture Market

Unified Pan-India Trading Platform

An online trading portal linking existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market, enabling farmers to trade commodities digitally for better prices.

Focus:

Price Transparency

Platform:

Digital e-Trading

Goal:

Market Integration

Benefit:

Higher Price Discovery

2016

Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive Crop Risk Management

Provides comprehensive crop insurances against non-preventable natural risks (e.g., pests, flood), ensuring income continuity during crop failure

Focus:

Disaster Protection

Premium:

Max 2% (Kharif) / 1.5% (Rabi)

Coverage:

Pre-sowing to Post-harvest

Benefit:

Income Stabilisation

2019

KUSUM Yojana

Solar Energy Integration & Independence

Promotes solar energy usage by subsidizing the installation of solar pumps and grid-connected solar power plants in rural areas for irrigation.

Focus:

Energy Independence

Support:

Subsidy for Solar Pumps

Benefit:

Lower Input Costs

Type:

Decarbonization

2019

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi

Direct Income Support to Farmers

Provides ‚Çπ6,000 per year to eligible landholding farmer families, transferred in 3 equal installments directly into their bank accounts.

Focus:

Income Stability

Transfer:

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

Frequency:

Quarterly Payments

Target:

All Landholders

2020

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Financial Support for Farmers

A nationwide drive launched in 2020 to provide Kisan Credit Cards to all eligible farmers. It offers affordable short-term loans, quick credit access.

Focus:

Affordable Credit Access

Support:

Low-Interest Loans (with 4% subsidy)

Benefit:

Instant Credit for Seeds & Fertilizers.

Target:

Crop Farmers, Fisheries Sector

2021

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

Financing for Farm Infrastructure

A long-term financing scheme that offers low-interest loans for building post-harvest and farming infrastructure such as warehouses, cold storage units and processing facilities

Focus:

Infrastructure Development

Support:

3% Interest Subvention

Coverage:

Storage, Supply-chain Assets

Benefit:

Better Crop Handling

2025

Digital Agriculture Mission

Technology Integration in Agriculture

Aims to introduce advanced technologies such as AI-based crop monitoring, drone spraying, satellite mapping, and smart sensors and also helps farmers in making data-driven decisions.

Focus:

Digital & Smart Farming

Support:

Subsidy on Digital Tools

Benefit:

Reduced Input Costs

Goal:

Widespread Adoption of Agri-Tech

2025

Green Irrigation Initiative

Sustainable & Climate-Friendly Irrigation

Promotes the use of solar-powered drip irrigation systems and water-efficient technologies in agriculture. Aims to reduce dependency on diesel pumps, save groundwater.

Focus:

Water-Efficient Irrigation

Support:

Subsidy on Pump Systems

Benefit:

Energy Savings

Goal:

Climate-Resilient Irrigation
